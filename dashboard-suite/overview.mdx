## Your Analytics Suite

Crux provides **seven core performance reports** designed to answer specific business questions. Each report combines data from multiple sources—Shopify, GA4, advertising platforms—into unified views that eliminate data reconciliation and enable immediate action.

<Info>
All reports include **statistical health indicators** that automatically flag unusual performance, helping you prioritise what needs attention each morning.
</Info>

## Core Business Reports

<CardGroup cols={2}>

<Card 
    title="Daily KPI Performance" 
    icon="chart-line" 
    href="/dashboard-suite/daily_kpi_performance"
  >
    **Your morning dashboard for business triage**
    
    Comprehensive daily view of core KPIs: marketing spend, sessions, orders, revenue, profitability (CM1/CM2/CM3), split by new vs. repeat customers and one-off vs. subscription orders.
    
    **Best for**: Daily performance monitoring, trend identification, CAC tracking, understanding new vs. repeat dynamics.
  </Card>

  <Card 
    title="Channel Performance" 
    icon="satellite-dish" 
    href="/dashboard-suite/channel_performance"
  >
    **Multi-channel marketing efficiency analysis**
    
    Daily performance across all marketing channels broken down by country, platform, campaign, and ad group. Complete profitability view with CM1/CM2/CM3 metrics.
    
    **Best for**: Budget allocation decisions, channel comparison, campaign optimisation, understanding acquisition efficiency by source.
  </Card>

  <Card 
    title="Orders" 
    icon="receipt" 
    href="/dashboard-suite/orders"
  >
    **Order-level analysis** with complete attribution
    
    Granular view of every DTC order including customer details, product data, financial metrics, discount attribution, and complete marketing channel assignment.
    
    **Best for**: Investigating specific orders, analysing discount code effectiveness, understanding product mix, validating attribution logic.
  </Card>

  <Card 
    title="Customer Deep Dive" 
    icon="users" 
    href="/dashboard-suite/customer_deep_dive"
  >
    **Customer-level lifetime value analysis**
    
    Every customer with first-order attribution, lifetime financial performance, product category preferences, and behavioural segmentation (New, Active, Lapsed, Churned).
    
    **Best for**: Understanding customer segments, identifying high-value cohorts, analysing retention patterns, building targeted strategies.
  </Card>

</CardGroup>

## Channel Deep Dive Reports

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card 
    title="Meta Performance" 
    icon="meta" 
    href="/dashboard-suite/meta_performance"
  >
    **Facebook & Instagram advertising analysis**
    
    Ad-level performance combining Meta platform metrics (impressions, clicks, CPM) with attributed orders, revenue, and profitability. Includes creative performance tracking with ad age analysis and winner identification.
    
    **Best for**: Creative testing, audience optimisation, understanding ad fatigue, identifying winning ad formats, CAC management.
    
    **Key features**: Ad velocity tracking, win rate analysis, weighted ad age monitoring, video engagement metrics.
  </Card>

  <Card 
    title="Paid Search Performance" 
    icon="magnifying-glass" 
    href="/dashboard-suite/paid_search_performance"
  >
    **Google Ads & Bing Ads campaign analysis**
    
    Campaign and ad group-level performance combining search platform metrics with session data and complete order attribution. Automatic campaign type classification (Brand, Generic, Shopping, Performance Max).
    
    **Best for**: Keyword optimisation, brand vs. non-brand efficiency comparison, shopping feed performance, budget allocation across search types.
    
    **Key features**: Campaign type segmentation, click-to-order attribution, profitability by search strategy.
  </Card>

  <Card 
    title="Influencer Performance" 
    icon="bullhorn" 
    href="/dashboard-suite/influencer_performance"
  >
    **Post-level influencer ROI tracking**
    
    Individual influencer post performance with discount code attribution, cumulative metrics, and automatic "winner" identification. Track costs, orders, profitability, and repeat collaboration effectiveness.
    
    **Best for**: Influencer ROI analysis, identifying top-performing creators, understanding which content styles convert, planning repeat collaborations.
    
    **Key features**: Post index tracking (1st, 2nd, 3rd+ collaborations), 7-day cumulative winner criteria, category and follower segmentation.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Specialty Reports

<CardGroup cols={2}>

  <Card 
    title="LTV & Retention" 
    icon="rotate" 
    href="/dashboard-suite/ltv_retention"
  >
    **Cohort lifetime value and retention analysis**
    
    36-month cohort tracking showing revenue, orders, and profitability evolution over time. Segment by acquisition channel, product category, country, discount usage, and order type.
    
    **Best for**: Understanding true customer value, payback period analysis, retention curve comparison across cohorts, LTV:CAC ratio calculation.
    
    **Key features**: Cohort segmentation across multiple dimensions, contribution margin tracking over time, retention curve visualisation.
  </Card>

</CardGroup>
