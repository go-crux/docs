## Onboarding Timeline

Crux implementation typically takes **2-3 weeks** from kickoff to production dashboards. Here's what to expect:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Week 1: Data Foundation">
    ### Data Access & COGS Implementation
    
    **What we need from you:**
    - Admin access to Shopify, Recharge, GA4, Meta Ads Manager, Google Ads, Bing Ads, & TikTok Ads Manager where relevant.
    - Marketing channel classification rules (how you define "Paid Social" vs "Organic Social").
    - COGS (Cost of Goods Sold) breakdown by product/SKU.
    - Fulfilment cost per order (broken down by order type).
    - Payment processing fee structure.
    - Affiliate marketing costs (if relevant).
    - Influencer posts, costs & follower / category stats.
    
    **What we deliver:**
    - Secure data pipeline setup connecting all sources to BigQuery.
    - Initial COGS model implementation based on your product economics.
    - Configuration sheet setup for your customisation needs (marketing channels, influencer tracking, discount code tracking).
    
    <Warning>
    Accurate COGS data is critical for profitability metrics. We'll work with you to ensure product costing reflects your actual economics.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Week 2: Configuration & Modelling">
    ### Channel Definition & Data Transformation
    
    **What we need from you:**
    - Any custom metrics or dimensions specific to your business.
    - Potential availability to answer any ad hoc integration questions.
    
    **What we deliver:**
    - Configured marketing channel attribution logic in your configuration sheet.
    - Complete DBT data models transforming raw data into analytics-ready tables.
    - Initial dashboard builds based on your core KPIs.
    
    <Info>
    The configuration sheet becomes your control panel for ongoing customisationâ€”no code changes required for channel updates.
    </Info>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Week 3: Dashboard Build & Kickoff">
    ### Dashboard Finalisation & Training
    
    **Kickoff call agenda:**
    - Walkthrough of all dashboard reports and their use cases.
    - Health indicator system explanation and interpretation.
    - Configuration sheet training for self-service updates.
    - Q&A and prioritisation of any additional customisation needs.
    
    **What you receive:**
    - Production-ready Looker Studio dashboards.
    - Access to your configuration sheet for ongoing updates.
    - Documentation covering all metrics and data definitions.
    - Direct support channel for questions.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What Happens Next

### Daily Operations

Once live, Crux becomes your morning routine:

1. **2-minute triage**: Review health indicators to see what needs attention.
2. **Deep dives**: Investigate flagged metrics using detailed reports.
3. **Action**: Spend less time playing data detective and make informed decisions backed by comprehensive data.

### Monthly Partnership

Your monthly fee includes 7 hours of:

- **Customisation**: New metrics, modified channel definitions, additional reports.
- **Analysis**: Deep-dive investigations into specific questions or opportunities.
- **Optimisation**: Dashboard refinements based on how you're using the platform. 

<Warning>
Monthly hours do not roll over into subsequent months if unused.
</Warning>
