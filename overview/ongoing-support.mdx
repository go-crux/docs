Crux isn't a "set it and forget it" platform, we're your ongoing growth partner. Every subscription includes **7 hours monthly of dashboard configuration** to ensure your analytics evolve with your business, plus access to strategic analysis services when you need deeper investigation.

## What's Included Monthly

### 7 Hours of Dashboard Configuration

Every month includes **7 hours** for dashboard adjustments and refinements as your business evolves:

  <Card title="Layout Adjustments" icon="table-layout">

**Common uses of included hours:**

- Add new product category breakdown to profitability dashboard
- Swap bar chart to line graph for better trend visibility
- Add country filter to channel performance report
- Adjust dashboard layout for mobile viewing
- Surface discount code performance in customer analysis
- Modify health indicator thresholds based on seasonal patterns

**Typical turnaround:** Same day for simple changes, 2-3 days for new dashboard views.

<Warning>
Monthly hours do not roll over into subsequent months if unused.
</Warning>

### Free Support (Doesn't Consume Hours)

Get help understanding your data without using your monthly allocation:

- **Dashboard interpretation**: Understanding metrics, reading health indicators, navigating reports
- **Configuration sheet guidance**: Help using the customisation spreadsheet for channel definitions
- **Data discrepancy investigations**: Resolving differences between platforms, troubleshooting data quality
- **Technical troubleshooting**: Dashboard loading issues, export problems, access questions
- **Best practice recommendations**: How to structure analysis, which reports to use when

<Tip>
If you're unsure whether a request uses your included hours or is free support, just ask—we'll tell you upfront before starting any work.
</Tip>

### Work That Requires Separate Billing

Beyond dashboard configuration and support, these requests are scoped and billed separately:

**Data Engineering Work:**
- New data models or calculated metrics not in existing data layer
- Attribution model updates requiring historical reclassification
- Connecting new data sources (Klaviyo, Gorgias, Fairing, affiliate networks, etc.)
- Custom CSV uploads and integration

**Custom Analysis:**
- Ad-hoc investigations requiring data exploration and analytical work
- Deep-dive analysis on specific questions or business problems
- Performance audits and strategic recommendations
- Executive presentations or board materials

<Warning>
All billable work is scoped and quoted upfront before we begin. If a project requires more hours than estimated, we discuss scope adjustment or seek additional approval—**no surprise invoices**.
</Warning>
